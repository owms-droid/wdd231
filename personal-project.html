Personal Portfolio Site Plan

This document outlines the plan for creating a personal portfolio website to showcase my projects and skills.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to present my work, skills, and experiences in a professional manner to potential employers and collaborators.

Scenarios for My Personal Portfolio

  • What is the most meaningful project you worked on?
  • What skills do you want to highlight?

Color Schema

The color scheme will be a combination of blue and white, with blue representing professionalism and trust, and white for clarity and simplicity.

Typography

The primary font will be 'Roboto' for its modern and clean look, with headings in bold to create a hierarchy of information.

Wireframe

The wireframe will include a header with navigation links, a main content area for showcasing projects, and a footer with contact information.
