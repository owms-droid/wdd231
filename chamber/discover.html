About Huanuco

Huánuco city offers a peek into Peru’s deep history. It was founded in 1539 by Spanish conquistador Gómez de Alvarado. The city still holds its colonial charm, with many buildings showing the Spanish influence. Walking through Huánuco feels like stepping back in time, letting you see its cultural heritage.

Source: Huanuco.gov
